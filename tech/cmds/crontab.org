* crontab
* Description
* Usage
- list the cron schedule
#+BEGIN_SRC sh
crontab -l
#+END_SRC

- edit the cron schedule
#+BEGIN_SRC sh
crontab -e
#+END_SRC

** Example
0 1 * * 1-5 /bin/execute/this/script 
  
minute - 0 (zero minute of the hour)
hour - 1 (1am)
day of month * (no matter the date of the month)
month - * (every month)
weekday - 1-5 (monday through friday)
