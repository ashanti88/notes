*Point to Point Protocol*

Data Link Layer protocol.

It establishes a link between 2 nodes.

It can provide authentication, encryption and compression.

PPP can be used over serial connection, phone line, trunk line, cellular
connection, radio links, fiber(SONET) and over IP.

2 Derivatives of PPP i) Point-to-Point Protocol over Ethernet ii)
Point-to-Point Protocol over ATM These two are commonly used by ISP to
establish a Digital Subscriber Line (DSL) connection for customers.
